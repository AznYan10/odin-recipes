Fried Rice Recipes

Description

Fried rice are one of my favorite dishes to eat, fairly simple to make and is very tasty

Recipes

  • Cooked Rice
  • oil/butter
  • vegatables
  • chicke/pork
  • Troasted Sesame Seed Oil
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Fish Sauce
  • Salt
  • (reccomends) Fried Eggs

Steps

  1. Heat the pan on high
  2. add butter/oil on pan
  3. Cut vegetables
  4. Cut meat
  5. Mix fish and oyseter sauce on a bowl
  6. Add the meat to the pan
  7. Remove from pan when cooked and place on plate
  8. Add the egg and place on plate when done
  9. Add the vegetabel to the pan and don't remove from the pan
  10. Add rice to pan
  11. Add the mix fish and oyster sauce to the pan
  12. Once it starts steaming add the rest of the ingredeints on the plate
