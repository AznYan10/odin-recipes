Fried Noodle Recipes

Description

Fried noodles is also another great dish that I love to eat

Recipes

  • Dried Noodles
  • Chicken/Pork/Shrimp
  • Eggs
  • vegatables
  • Oil/butter
  • Soy Sauce
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Rice Vineger
  • Hoisin Sauce
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Put water in put and cook noodles on high heat till soft
  2. When noodles are soft place them on a plate
  3. While noodle is boiling add oil to pan on high and wait for it to steam
  4. After pan steams add meat to it
  5. When meat is cooked remove from pan and place on a place
  6. Add oil to pan again and cook the eggs and place them on the plate when done
  7. Then add more oil to the pan and start cooking the vegetables
  8. When vegatables are soft add the noodles and all the sauces to the pan
  9. Cook for about 3min and then add the meat and eggs to the pan
  10. Cook for another 3min and then turn off the stove
  11. Can now serve the noodles
