Baked Chicken Thighs Recipes

Description

Baked chicken thighs are simple to make and they taste amazing. Easy to make when I don't really want to cook

Recipes

  • Chicken thighs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Olive Oil
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Cayenne Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degree
  2. Put all the ingredeints on a bowl
  3. Mix the bowl well for 30 seconds
  4. Mix the ingredeints with the chicken thighs
  5. Place the chicken on a big metal plate
  6. Make sure the oven is hot and close to 400 degree
  7. Put the chicken in the oven
  8. Put timer for 40min and wait for the chicken
  9. Afer 40min take out the chicken from the oven and leave for a few minutes to cool
  10. Can serve after it cools for a few minutes
